# Reviewer Questions

## What happens if evidence is wrong?
Verification will fail due to Merkle mismatch.

## How do you prevent cherry-picking evidence?
All evidence is committed before verification.

## How does this relate to formal verification?
This complements formal methods by adding reproducibility.

## Can this be applied beyond QKD?
Yes. The framework is protocol-agnostic.

## What are the limitations?
- Not a full formal proof system
- Depends on correct evidence generation
